Asset Diary ("we") respects and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Asset Diary does not collect, upload, or store any of your personal information or asset data.

All data is stored solely on your local device. We do not provide cloud storage services, nor do we sync your data to any servers.

You may export your data via the App's built‑in export feature, allowing you to back up your data locally on your own.

Uninstalling the App will delete all data stored locally. We cannot remotely access or delete your data—you remain in full control at all times.
